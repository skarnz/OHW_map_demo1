## Using This Education Library

### For Patients
- Start with modules most relevant to your current treatment plan
- Complete knowledge checks to reinforce learning
- Discuss questions with your healthcare provider
- Apply practical tips and strategies gradually

### For Healthcare Providers
- Use modules to supplement patient education
- Assign specific modules based on patient needs
- Review knowledge check results with patients
- Customize education plans using module content

## Module Features

Each module includes:
- **Learning Objectives**: Clear goals for what you'll learn
- **Comprehensive Content**: Evidence-based information in accessible language
- **Practical Examples**: Real-world applications and tips
- **Key Takeaways**: Summary of essential points
- **Knowledge Check**: Multiple-choice questions with answer keys
- **Visual Aids**: Analogies and examples to clarify complex concepts

## Continuous Learning

This education library is designed to support ongoing learning throughout your weight loss journey. Return to modules as needed, and don't hesitate to discuss any questions or concerns with your healthcare team.
