# OHW Build Features - Education & Engagement Extract

## Education & Engagement Features

### Micro-Learning Modules
- **MyPlate App Reference**: The MyPlate app offers badges for achieving goals, aligning with the OHW vision
- **Gamification Elements**: Badge system for goal achievement
- **Goal-Oriented Learning**: Education tied to specific health goals

### Community Content & Video Series
- **Stanford Video Library**: Current library of video-taped lecture series created at Stanford
- **Live Q&A Sessions**: Available for patient engagement
- **Resource**: https://healthlibrary.stanford.edu/video/diabetes-video.html
- **Future Expansion**: Plans to expand community content offerings

### Educational Integration Points

#### 1. Weekly Assessment Questions
- Delivered via app with push notifications 24-48 hours before appointments
- Educational topics covered:
  - Fitness minutes tracking
  - Weight management
  - Nutrition education
  - Side effects management
  - Mental health awareness
  - Challenge identification
  - Medication education
  - HCP question categorization

#### 2. Nutrition Education Framework
- **USDA MyPlate Integration**: 
  - Portion size estimation education
  - Hand measurement guidelines
  - EBT card option for low-income patients
- **Recipe Education**: 28-day menu generation based on macronutrient requirements
- **Sustainable Cooking Education**: Focus on teaching patients to cook for themselves

#### 3. SMART Goals Education
- Document outlining SMART goals framework
- How to present the concept to patients
- Initial and updated smart goals in both patient and provider dashboards
- Educational approach to goal setting and achievement

#### 4. CDC Educational Requirements
- Initial sessions capture demographics for CDC requirements
- Education on program qualification criteria
- Ongoing tracking education for:
  - Fitness minutes
  - Weight management
  - Health metrics

### Educational Content Delivery Methods

1. **In-App Modules**: Micro-learning content delivered through the mobile app
2. **Video Content**: Stanford lecture series and future video content
3. **Interactive Assessments**: Weekly questions that educate while gathering data
4. **Visual Learning**: Photo documentation for meal education and habit awareness
5. **Community Learning**: Group sessions and live Q&A opportunities

### Future Educational Considerations

1. **Wearable Integration Education**: Teaching patients to use and interpret data from wearables
2. **Lab Result Education**: OCR capability to help patients understand their lab results
3. **Behavioral Change Education**: Focus on sustainability of change
4. **Provider Accessibility**: Education that helps patients communicate effectively with providers

### Educational Calendar Integration
Participants can opt into calendar features for:
- Education module reminders
- Virtual check-in sessions
- Community educational events
- Lab result review sessions

### Key Educational Principles Identified
1. **Sustainability Focus**: Education aimed at long-term behavior change
2. **Accessibility**: Content designed for various socioeconomic backgrounds
3. **Engagement**: Gamification and badges to maintain interest
4. **Practical Application**: Hands-on learning (cooking, portion control)
5. **Provider Integration**: Education that supports provider-patient communication
