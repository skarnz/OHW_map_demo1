# OHW Patient Education Library - Table of Topics

## Overview
This document outlines the complete patient education library content organized into modules for the OHW (Operation Health and Wellness) platform. Each topic includes content description and possible usage scenarios.

## Education Topics

### 1. Inventory for Starting a Weight Loss and Wellness Journey
- **Content**: Overview of key considerations and steps for starting a weight loss journey
- **Usage Scenario**: Initial consultation with patients beginning their weight loss journey

### 2. Success Strategies for Utilizing Weight Loss Medications
- **Content**: Tips and strategies for effectively using weight loss medications
- **Usage Scenario**: Follow-up appointment to discuss medication adherence and effectiveness

### 3. Semaglutide Overview
- **Content**: Information on Semaglutide, its benefits, and potential side effects
- **Usage Scenario**: Patient education session on new medication options

### 4. Fitness Plan for Weight Loss
- **Content**: Guidelines for creating a personalized fitness plan
- **Usage Scenario**: One-on-one coaching session to develop an exercise routine

### 5. Meal Prep: Weight Loss Made Easy
- **Content**: Simple meal prep ideas and recipes for weight loss
- **Usage Scenario**: Group workshop on meal planning and preparation

### 6. Balanced Eating and Nutritional Supplements
- **Content**: Overview of balanced eating and the role of supplements
- **Usage Scenario**: Nutritional counseling session to discuss dietary needs

### 7. Increasing GLP-1 Naturally
- **Content**: Natural ways to boost GLP-1 levels through diet and lifestyle
- **Usage Scenario**: Patient education on lifestyle changes to enhance medication effects

### 8. Nutritional Supplements
- **Content**: Information on various nutritional supplements and their benefits
- **Usage Scenario**: Discussion during a follow-up visit about supplement options

### 9. Probiotics and Your Gut Health
- **Content**: The role of probiotics in gut health and weight management
- **Usage Scenario**: Educational session on gut health and its impact on weight loss

### 10. Management of Digestive Issues on Weight Loss Medications

### 15. Intermittent Fasting and Weight Loss

### 16. Insulin Hormone Impact on Weight Loss

- **Content**: Guidelines for safely tapering off GLP-1 medications
- **Usage Scenario**: Consultation to discuss medication adjustments and patient concerns

## Key Observations

### Module Organization
- Topics are organized as individual modules that can be delivered independently
- Each module is designed to address specific patient needs and scenarios
- Content progression moves from basic concepts to more advanced topics

### Content Categories
1. **Getting Started**: Topics 1-3 (Initial journey, medication strategies, Semaglutide overview)
2. **Lifestyle & Nutrition**: Topics 4-9 (Fitness, meal prep, supplements, gut health)
3. **Medical Management**: Topics 10-13, 20-21 (Side effects, hormones, injections, tapering)
4. **Advanced Concepts**: Topics 14-19 (Plateaus, fasting, insulin, CGM, pre-diabetes)

### Educational Approach
- Practical, actionable content
- Mix of individual and group learning scenarios
- Focus on both medication management and lifestyle modifications
- Emphasis on patient empowerment and self-management
