## Landing Page Messaging Considerations

### Alternative Welcome Messages
Instead of "Your health journey starts here," consider:

1. **Begin your path to wellness right here.**
2. **Step into your wellness adventure now.**
3. **Your road to a healthier you begins here.**
4. **Start your quest for well-being today.**
5. **Embark on your wellness journey from this point forward.**
6. **Take the first step towards a healthier life here.**
7. **Your adventure in wellness starts now.**
8. **Launch your journey to better health today.**
9. **Begin your transformation to wellness right here.**

## Key Landing Page Elements to Consider

### After Initial Landing Page
- Link that opens to questionnaire/assessment
- Progressive disclosure of information
- Clear call-to-action buttons

### Common Features Across Examples
- Clear value propositions
- Social proof/testimonials
- Simple sign-up process
- Mobile-responsive design
- Performance tracking parameters
- Route-based personalization options
