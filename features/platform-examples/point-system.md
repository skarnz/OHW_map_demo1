# OHW Point System

## Overview
OHW likes the point system that Weight Watchers has defined and wants to incorporate points into activities. Participants can attain points by completing daily and weekly tasks.

## Point-Earning Activities

### Daily & Weekly Tasks
- Exercise completion
- Stress management activities
- Sleep goals achievement
- Planning meals
- Trying new recipes
- Attaining a SMART Goal
- Achieving intermittent fasting goals
- Weekly weigh-in
- Reviewing weekly lifestyle change education modules
- Extra points for reviewing additional education
- Extra points for additional health tasks
- Community activities or posting in the OHW community

### Health Milestone Points
- Attainment of improved wellness milestones (decreased risk of mortality milestones)
- Decrease in BMI
- Decrease in Obesity stage
- A1c decrease
- Attainment of stable lipid profile
- Stable blood pressure

## The OHW Point System Process

### How It Works

#### 1. Personalized Points (Operational Plan)
Upon joining OHW, participants complete an assessment that evaluates:
- Age
- Weight
- Height
- Sex assigned at birth
- Activity level
- Weight loss goals

This information determines personalized:
- KCal range
- Fitness needs
- Timeline to achieve weight goal

#### 2. Tracking Points
- Utilize the OHW app to log Points for food and drinks consumed daily
- App features an extensive food database
- Simplified tracking process

#### 3. Staying Within Your Operation Plan
- Objective: remain within daily Kcal budget
- Encourages healthier food choices
- Better understanding of portion sizes
- Fitness logging calculates into daily expenditure

#### 4. Weekly Points and Rollover
- Weekly Points allowance for special occasions
- Use when exceeding daily limit
- Unused daily Points (up to 4) can roll over into weekly Operation budget

## Benefits of the Points System

### Simplifies Nutrition Information
- Makes complex nutritional data manageable
- Tracks Macro Nutrients, Kcals including:
  - Carbohydrates (CHO)
  - Protein
  - Fats

### Encourages Healthy Choices
- Tracks food intake and exercise
- Monitors ongoing totals
- Helps understand how diet, exercise, sleep, and stress interact
- Shows collective impact on health

### Promotes Portion Control
- Tracking Kcals and exercise with ongoing totals
- Fosters awareness of portion sizes
- Allows members to make necessary adjustments

### Flexibility
- Guides toward healthier eating and fitness
- Allows for flexibility
- Enables enjoyment of treats while maintaining balanced diet

### Tracking Support
- OHW app assistance
- Community support systems
- Helps members stay on track
- Develops healthier habits
