# Progress Bars & Levels

## Progress Bars
- **Daily Progress Bar**: Track daily achievements and goals
- **Weekly Progress Bar**: Monitor weekly progress and milestones
- **Monthly Progress Bar**: View monthly accomplishments
- **Total Program Progress Bar**: Overall program completion tracking

## Levels System
- **Level 1**: 10,000 points - levels unlock new screens and user experience
- **Level 2**: 20,000 points
- **Level 3**: 30,000 points
- And so on...

## Challenges & Quests

### Available Challenge Categories
- **Fitness/Activity**: Physical activity challenges
- **Food/Hydration**: Nutrition and water intake goals
- **Learning**: Educational content completion
- **Weight**: Weight management objectives
- **Sleep**: Sleep quality and duration goals
- **Self-reflection/Stress Reduction**: Mental wellness activities
- **Food Swap Game**: Interactive healthy food substitution challenges
- **Weigh-ins**: Regular weight tracking milestones
- **Food Tracking**: Consistent meal logging
- **Fitness Tracking**: Exercise and activity monitoring
- **Participation in Community**: Engagement with other users
- **Self-study**: Reading learning articles and finishing knowledge checks

## Rewards System

### Points
- Earn points for completing challenges and activities

### Discounts & Partnerships
- Gym membership discounts
- Clothing store promotions
- Fitness company partnerships
- Grocery store discounts (Safeway/Kroger's)
- In-office rewards/discounts specific to the medical practice

## Social Features
- **Opt-in Competition**: Compete against friends
- **Social Sharing**: Share accomplishments/experiences on social media
- **Collaboration Challenges**: Work together to achieve common goals/group challenges
- **Community Building**: Access to community forum for support and discussion
