# Weight Tracking App Dashboard

## Overview
At-a-glance weight tracking app dashboard for participants

## Key Features

### Weight Tracking
- Clear visualization of your weight over time
- Charts and graphs showing your progress
- Historical data presentation

### Calorie/Macro Tracking
- Display of daily calorie goals
- Remaining calorie balance
- Macronutrient breakdowns:
  - Protein
  - Carbohydrates
  - Fats

### Activity Tracking
- Integration with fitness trackers
- Ability to log workouts manually
- Display of calorie expenditure

### Goal Setting and Progress
- Visual representation of weight loss goals
- Progress tracking towards achieving goals
- Milestone indicators

### Customization
- Ability to tailor the dashboard
- Display information most relevant to individual goals
- Personalized metrics and views
