# OHW Journey Map - 12 Month Patient Journey

## Overview
This document outlines the 12-month patient journey for the OHW (Optimal Health & Wellness) weight management program, including both GLP-1 medication patients and DPP (Diabetes Prevention Program) participants.

## Journey Structure

### Visit Types
- **NPV**: New Patient Visit (30 minutes)
- **RPV**: Return Patient Visit (15 minutes)

### Visit Frequency
- **Weeks 0-8**: Weekly visits (RPV 15)
- **Weeks 9-16**: Bi-weekly visits (RPV 15)
- **Months 5-12**: Monthly visits (RPV 15-30)

## Key Journey Phases

### Phase 1: Initial Intake & Onboarding (Week 0)
#### Front Desk (FD) Tasks:
- Provide initial questionnaire (QNR)
- Confirm patient contacts
- Confirm financial details
- Initiate lab intake
- Confirm labs/QNR response uploaded to patient chart
- For DPP Program: Launch different intake process

#### Healthcare Team (HCT) Tasks:
- Obtain patient consent
- Conduct assessment/review labs
- Weight/Waist/Photo measurements
- Review travel/life events/goal timeline
- Introduce program
- Initial goal setting with patient
- Initiate tracking tools
- If above complete: 1st dose administration
- Document using 5A framework

### Phase 2: Weekly Sessions (Visits 1-8)
#### Standard Visit Protocol (5A Framework):
1. Weight/Waist measurements (Photo on visits 4 & 8)
2. Fitness minutes review
3. Food tracking review
4. Goal review
5. Data analysis
6. Dose review
7. Medication administration
8. HCT documentation

#### Front Desk Support:
- Schedule appointments (3 appointments if on medication, 1 if not)
- Send follow-up confirmations and reminders

#### Microlearning (ML) Topics by Week:
- **Week 1**: Jump Start Your Activity Plan, Track fitness minutes
- **Week 2**: Healthy Eating, Food log tracking
- **Week 3**: Move Those Muscles, Being Active - A Way of Life
- **Week 4**: Tip the Calorie Balance, Burn More Calories Than You Take In
- **Week 5**: Get More Active
- **Week 6**: Shop to Cook and Prevent Weight Loss
- **Week 7**: Four Keys to Healthy Eating Out
- **Week 8**: Problem Solving, Injecting GLP-1 and safe storage

### Phase 3: Bi-Weekly Sessions (Visits 9-16)
#### Enhanced Protocol:
- Continue 5A framework assessments
- Medication distribution (vs. administration)
- Lab slip provided at Visit 15
- Review next steps tailored to patient goals/progress

#### Microlearning Topics:
- Stress management and its impact on weight loss
- When weight loss stalls
- Environmental control
- Motivation strategies
- Heart health
- Support systems
- Fat and calorie reduction techniques

### Phase 4: Monthly Maintenance (Visits 17-22)
#### Comprehensive Monthly Reviews:
- Photo documentation each visit
- 1-month data analysis
- Maintenance plan determination
- Medication titration/graduation decisions
- Milestone reviews (Visit 22)

#### Advanced Microlearning:
- Physical activity advancement
- Time management
- Nutritional variety and balance
- Holiday/vacation/special event management
- Reflection and future planning

## Key Milestones & Touchpoints

### Clinical Touchpoints:
1. **Initial Assessment**: Comprehensive intake with labs, measurements, and goal setting
2. **Week 4 Check-in**: First photo comparison, dose adjustment
3. **Week 8 Review**: Progress evaluation, next phase planning
4. **Week 15 Labs**: Follow-up laboratory work
5. **Month 6 Milestone**: Comprehensive review and maintenance planning
6. **Month 12 Graduation**: Final assessment and long-term planning

### Patient Education Journey:
1. **Foundation Building** (Weeks 0-4): Basic tracking, activity, and nutrition
2. **Skill Development** (Weeks 5-8): Advanced strategies and problem-solving
3. **Behavior Modification** (Months 3-6): Stress management, motivation, environmental control
4. **Maintenance Preparation** (Months 7-12): Long-term sustainability and independence

### Data Collection Points:
- Weight and waist measurements: Every visit
- Photo documentation: Weeks 0, 4, 8, then monthly
- Lab work: Initial and Week 15
- Fitness minutes: Weekly tracking
- Food logs: Daily tracking reviewed weekly/bi-weekly/monthly
- Goal progress: Continuous assessment

## Support Systems

### 24/7 Escalation Pathway:
- TBD escalation pathway to clinic for follow-up outreach
- AI-powered microlearning modules available on-demand
- App-based support for questions and challenges

### Educational Module Categories:
1. **Initial Challenges**: Goal setting, habit assessment, barrier identification
2. **Nutrition & Eating**: Portion control, carb counting, healthy substitutions
3. **Physical Activity**: Cardio vs. strength training, home workouts, consistency
4. **Behavior Change**: Long-term strategies, environment setup, social support
5. **Weight Loss Science**: Understanding metabolism, plateaus, body composition
6. **Lifestyle Integration**: Meal planning, social pressure, celebration strategies

## Program Variations

### GLP-1 Medication Track:
- Medication administration/distribution at each visit
- Injection training and storage education
- Dose titration based on progress
- Side effect management

### DPP (Diabetes Prevention Program) Track:
- CDC-approved curriculum integration
- Different intake process
- Focus on diabetes prevention education
- Modified microlearning sequence

## Success Metrics
- Weight loss percentage
- Waist circumference reduction
- Fitness minutes achievement
- Food tracking compliance
- Goal attainment
- Program completion rate
- Maintenance success (6+ months post-program)
